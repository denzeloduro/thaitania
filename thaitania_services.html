Our Services


Due to the COVID-19 Pandemic, our vibrant community has had to isolate and distance ourselves from each other socially. The team at Thaitation understands how difficult these times can be for our loyal customers and have therefore introduce new services that can foster a feeling of togetherness.

Open Patio With Heat Lamps (Monday - Thursday):

To supplement our 40% opening capacity, we are setting up our patios to accomodate 25% more people to be able to welcome as many of our loyal customers as possible..


Free Delivery (Fridays only):

Same Thaitania treats delivered to the comfort of your homes for free. Applies to residents within a three-mile radius including Somerville, Medford, Cambridge and some parts of the greater Boston area.


Zoom Hangouts With A Local DJ (Saturdays only)

On Saturday nights, we bring the joyful ambience of our restaurant to you no matter where you are. Join Michelle and some of the Thaitation staff in a virtual bonding experience. This is made possible in collaboration with a local DJ.


Pad-Thai-On-The-Go (Sundays Only)

The crowd-favorite pad thai is hot, sumptious and ready for pick-up between 4pm to 6pm. Each bag has a small bottle of hibiscus beer as a treat.
